About Me

Hi, my name is Matthew and I am an aspiring data analyst. My pursuit of a career in this field is a recent endeavor; however, I have been interested in observing strange trends in data and striking visualizations from a young age. Whether it be puzzling results from a complex math problem, dramatic graphical representations of seemingly banal data, or statistical anomalies related to my favorite sports teams, I have always been drawn to numbers and their derived patterns and hypothetical conclusions.

I am a graduate of Transylvania University in Lexington, KY, with majors in Mathematics and Spanish Language and Literature. I gravitated towards both of these specialties for their underlying similarities of logic, recognizing patterns, and problem solving. My education and technical mindset have established my predisposition to learn coding, which I understand as a marriage between language and logic. I am self-taught in SQL, Excel, and Tableau and I desire to hone those skills, as well as advance in knowledge of new skills such as Python and Power BI. I recently earned my certificate in Googleâ€™s Data Analytics course on Coursera and I look forward to taking additional courses in this field in the future. My personal aspirations for continuous learning and perpetual improvement would be valuable assets for any data analytics team.

Previously, my work experience was in the field of manufacturing. For four years, I worked as a CNC Machinist in a specialty retail fixture shop, where I gained knowledge in streamlining production processes as well as technical skills such as programming machinery and understanding machine coding. More recently, I worked as a subcontractor with a small family business, where I became proficient in a wide array of remodeling skills. While these labor-intensive jobs seem unrelated to my current pursuit, the skills I acquired in them such as attention to detail, diligent planning, and efficient problem solving overlap and directly translate to the realm of data analysis. I am a former remodeler, looking to remodel his career.
